# SAT Tracker - Your Complete SAT Preparation Companion

SAT Tracker is a comprehensive mobile application designed to help students prepare effectively for the SAT exam. Built with React Native and powered by Supabase, this app provides a complete suite of tools to organize your study sessions, track progress, and stay motivated throughout your SAT preparation journey.

## What is SAT Tracker?

SAT Tracker is your personal study assistant that helps you master the SAT through smart planning, focused study sessions, and consistent progress tracking. Whether you're just starting your SAT prep or looking to improve your score, this app provides all the tools you need in one place.

## Core Features

### Study Planning System
Create customized study plans for Math, Reading, and Writing sections. Set your target dates, allocate study hours, and organize your preparation schedule efficiently. The app helps you break down your SAT prep into manageable daily tasks.

### Pomodoro Focus Timer
Stay focused during study sessions with our built-in Pomodoro timer. Study in focused intervals with automatic breaks to maintain peak concentration and avoid burnout. Available for all users.

### Study Room
Access a dedicated study environment with progress tracking and session history. Monitor your daily study streaks and see how many hours you've invested in your SAT preparation.

### Check-In System
Log your daily study sessions and track your progress. Mark topics as completed, review your study history, and maintain accountability with daily check-ins.

### Progress Analytics
View detailed statistics about your study patterns, including total study hours, session counts, and improvement trends across different SAT sections.

### Profile Customization
Set your education level, target score, and study preferences. Adjust notification settings and customize your experience to match your learning style.
